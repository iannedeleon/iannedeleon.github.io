This sculpture, made of found material, experimented with combining two different objects using materials and textures. The overall sculpture alludes to the want and need to be perfect despite being thrown into a pit of obstacles. The repetition of objects shows that problems will constantly occur in life unexpectedly. Untitled is a piece that brings out the inner struggles and effects that we hide away. We put on a facade as if all is okay and that our lives are perfect. This piece is meant to be a reminder that sometimes we are not okay and it helps to visualize what we are going through to understand and get through it.

Untitled 2017

Styrofoam, charcoal, stone, nails, thread, plastic
13" x 8" x 7"
