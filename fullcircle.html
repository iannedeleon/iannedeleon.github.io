Full Circle shows the completion of the human body. I have done works pertaining to only the bust, but never the body. This animation is the combination of three elements forming together to make a single unit. I wanted to show the transitions of being absorbed by something or experiencing events that make us stronger and a better person. In the beginning, the headless human feels anxiety in the midst of three intimidating busts. It ends with the headless human absorbing aspects of each bust to make the final figure.
