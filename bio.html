BIOGRAPHY

Ianne De Leon is a Filipino-American mixed media artist from Hayward, California. In 2020, she received her Bachelor of Fine Arts Degree in Digital Media Art from San Jose State University. In her work, De Leon expresses emotional ideas inspired by life events that leads to personal growth. Mainly working in 3d modeling and animation, she utilizes found digital objects and the technical processes of combining digital mediums to show seamless technical evolution. She hopes to communicate the idea that all emotions and experiences are valued and that we should not be afraid to express them.
